Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about diagnostic tests, appointment scheduling, and reports.

How can I book a home blood sample collection?
You can book a home sample collection by adding your required tests to the shopping cart, clicking checkout, and scheduling your patient details, address, and desired date/time slot. Our phlebotomist will visit you at the scheduled slot.
What is a diagnostic "Smart Report"?
Our Smart Reports simplify clinical jargon. They display color-coded indicators for your test values (Normal, Borderline, High), historic test graphs to track parameters overtime, and advice recommendations curated by medical experts.
Are the lab technicians certified?
Yes, all our phlebotomists and lab staff hold certified DMLT (Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology) or BMLT degrees and undergo regular quality training in sterile extraction processes.
